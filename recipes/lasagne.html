Lasagne

Dough:

  1. ¼ cup warm water
  2. 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  3. 1 cup cold water
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 3 cups bread flour
  6. 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Toppings:

  1. 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  2. ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  3. ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  4. ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  5. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  6. 6 leaves fresh basil, torn
